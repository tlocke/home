Priority Of Pensions If Company Goes Into Liquidation

Mon 12 August 2013

Opinion seems divided on whether it's a good thing that the UK Supreme Court has ruled that pensioners don't get priority if a company goes into liquidation. I like the idea of the stakeholder pension, which sidesteps this problem.
