The Restful Elected

Wed 29 November 2006

I've released a new version of The Elected. Have a look and let me know what you think. I've reworked it a bit to make it more Restful. The thing about The Elected is that it's a wiki, but it's also structured. Whereas Wikipedia is a wiki of a filesystem, The Elected is a wiki of a relational database. Anyway, tell me if you have any suggestions or anything.
