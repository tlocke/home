2024 Shaftesbury Mews Sheds

The idea of this page is to document the ideas of what to do with the two sheds (bike shed and recycling shed) which have become dilapidated and unsafe. Then maybe have a vote to see which one gets carried out?

  • Cost: £25k (£5k per shed)
